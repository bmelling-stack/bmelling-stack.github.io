Website Evaluations

Good Website Example (name: unseen.co)

I found this website in a competition for best websites, aparently it was a winner for a 2023 design competition. First thought: its incredible, didn't know this was possible

  • Contrast: Colors on the page are all distinct and contrast well. Looks very nice easy to understand. The best way to describe this is beautiful
  • Repetition: Layout of the page is consistent with all buttons being around the borders. They are also separated on category, (ex: top right is info)
  • Alignment: All things border-edge aligned. Things that aren't near the border in a neat fashion are dead center to capture users attention.
  • Proximity: All content that relates to each other are grouped together in the border edges, center content is realted and makes sense. Very pretty page.

Final Remarks: This page is insanely beautiful, the cursor influence the page while not being too distracting. All content and colors are beautiful as well. This makes sense on how it won a contest.

Bad Website (name: the big ugly website)

This page is unbelievably ugly. It was definitely made ugly on purpose as an example on a really bad crappy webpage. They did a good job on what a website should never look like.

  • Contrast: Yellow bright blinding background, black highlighted text, ugly colors everywhere. Also a bright purpose in display. It honestly hurts to look at with max brightness on.
  • Repetition: There is some repition with headers being present, but they're all unaligned, random colors, doesn't make sense, incredibly unstructured as well. No good repitition
  • Alignment: Literally nothing is aligned. Random alignment and things everywhere, photos that aren't loading. Random color boxes in unaligned places. Terrible everything
  • Proximity: Everything is way too cluttered! Two main columns that have content are too close together on the page. All content is not in any neat fashion. Super ugly

Final Remarks: This was definitely made to be ugly on purpose. Hard to look at, terrible in very crap category. Good example of what not to do for a webpage in any regard.
